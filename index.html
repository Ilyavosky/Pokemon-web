Wolfe Glick (Aliases: Wolfey, WolfeyGlick) is an American Pokémon VGC player and a content creator for M80 (Previously Beastcoast). He is the 2016 Pokémon VGC World Champion.

After winning the 2025 Toronto Regional Championships, Wolfe also became the first player to win 10 Regional Championships.

The 2025 Pokémon VGC Toronto Regional Championships is a video game Regional Championship tournament held in Toronto, Canada.
